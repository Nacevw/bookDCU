bookDCU couldn't be simpler!

bookDCU enables students to reserve a seat within a lab so they have a space to complete their projects or assignments.
Booking your seat can be done in 4 simple steps

  • 1
    Choose Date & Time

    Select the date and time you want to book for above.

  • 2
    Choose your room & seat

    Select your prefered lab and seat in the lab and confirm your choice.

  • 3
    Confirm your name

    Confirm your name as it appears on your DCU Google account.

  • 4
    Verify your booking

    At the time of your booking, select the booking to verify and upload an image of the QR code at your seat.
